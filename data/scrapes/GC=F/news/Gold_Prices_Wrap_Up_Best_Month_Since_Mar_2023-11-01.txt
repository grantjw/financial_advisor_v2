title:Gold_Prices_Wrap_Up_Best_Month_Since_March
The front-month gold futures contract rose 7.4% to $1,985.20 a troy ounce in October, its best monthly performance since March, when the [banking-sector turmoil](https://www.wsj.com/articles/banking-worries-fuel-gold-price-rally-66d0fc6d) and worries about the economy sent gold prices rising near record levels.
