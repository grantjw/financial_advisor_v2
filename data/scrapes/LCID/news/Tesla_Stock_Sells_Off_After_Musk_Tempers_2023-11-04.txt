title:Tesla_Stock_Sells_Off_After_Musk_Tempers_Cybertruck_Hopes_Other_EV_Makers_Drop
Tesla shares tumbled Thursday after Chief Executive [Elon Musk tempered expectations](https://www.wsj.com/business/earnings/tesla-tsla-q3-earnings-report-2023-ed3cee6f) for the company’s new Cybertruck. “It's a great product, but financially, it will take, I don't know, a year to 18 months before it is a significant positive cash flow contributor,” Musk told investors late Wednesday.
