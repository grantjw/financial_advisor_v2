Charter Communications stock is falling despite an earnings beat. Charter (ticker: CHTR), which operates under the Spectrum brand, posted third-quarter earnings of $8.25 a share, surpassing the $8.06 per share analysts surveyed by FactSet expected. The stock fell 5.3% to $389.85 in premarket trading.
