title:StartUp_Polestar_Updated_Its_Guidance_Its_Another_Bleak_Sign_for_EVs
Polestar Automotive provided an updated business plan to investors on Wednesday. For fiscal year 2025, Polestar (ticker: PSNY) now expects to generate gross profit margins in the “high-teens” and an annual volume of about 160,000 cars. In 2022, Polestar said it would ship about 290,000 units annually and generate gross profit margins north of 20%.
