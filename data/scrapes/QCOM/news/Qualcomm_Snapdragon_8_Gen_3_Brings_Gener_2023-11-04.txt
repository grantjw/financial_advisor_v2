Qualcomm Inc. (Nasdaq: QCOM) unveiled its latest premium mobile platform, the Snapdragon 8 Gen 3, at the annual Snapdragon Summit in Hawaii. This marks a significant shift from previous iterations as it is the first mobile platform designed with generative AI as its core focus.
The Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 is set to be integrated into flagship devices by global OEMs and renowned smartphone brands, including ASUS, Honor, OnePlus, Sony, Xiaomi, and more.
The new Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 is poised to usher in a new era of generative AI, enabling users to create unique content, enhance productivity, and unlock groundbreaking applications.
This mobile platform integrates high-performance AI throughout the system, promising premium performance and novel mobile experiences for consumers.
