title:Starbucks_to_grow_to_55000_global_stores_by_2030_sees_3_bln_in_cost_savings
Nov 2 (Reuters) - Starbucks is planning to expand its global store footprint to 55,000 stores by 2030, CEO Laxman Narasimhan said on Thursday.
The coffee chain is also targeting cost savings of over $3 billion over the next three years, Narasimhan said while speaking at the company's 2023 "Reinvention Update and Holiday Launch" conference. (Reporting by Deborah Sophia in Bengaluru; Editing by Anil D'Silva)
