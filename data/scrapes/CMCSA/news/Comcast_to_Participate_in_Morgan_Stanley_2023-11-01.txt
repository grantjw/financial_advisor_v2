PHILADELPHIA, October 30, 2023--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Comcast Corporation (Nasdaq: CMCSA) announced that on Thursday, November 16, 2023, Jason Armstrong, Chief Financial Officer of Comcast Corporation, will participate in the Morgan Stanley European Technology, Media & Telecom Conference.
A live webcast of the event will be available on the Company's Investor Relations website at www.cmcsa.com on Thursday, November 16, 2023, at 6:20 A.M. Eastern Time. An on-demand replay will be available shortly after the conclusion of the presentation.
