Tesla (ticker: TSLA), Palantir Technologies (PLTR), Advanced Micro Devices (AMD), and Ford Motor (F) were the most popular stocks by volume from Oct. 30 to Nov. 6, according to Dow Jones Market Data. Tesla had a cumulative volume of 739 million shares as the stock gained 11% during that time frame. The recent gains followed disappointing third-quarter financial results from the electric-vehicle maker that hit the stock heavily.
