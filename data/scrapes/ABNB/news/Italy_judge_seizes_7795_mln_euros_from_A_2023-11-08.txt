title:Italy_judge_seizes_7795_mln_euros_from_Airbnb_in_tax_probe
ROME, Nov 6 (Reuters) - An Italian judge has ordered the seizure of 779.5 million euros ($836.40 million) from short-term rentals platform Airbnb for alleged tax evasion, a statement from the Milan prosecutor's office said on Monday. ($1 = 0.9320 euros) (Reporting by Emilio Parodi, writing by Alvise Armellini, Editing by Chris Reese)
