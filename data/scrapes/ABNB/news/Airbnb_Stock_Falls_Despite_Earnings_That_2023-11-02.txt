Airbnb posted better-than-expected third-quarter financial results, but provided fourth-quarter guidance that fell a little below Street estimates. The results provide an early look at the state of the travel industry, ahead of coming reports from Expedia Booking.com and other players. Gross bookings were $18.3 billion, up 17% from a year ago, and above consensus at $17.9 billion.
